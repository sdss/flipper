Education/Public Outreach

SDSS Home
The Sloan Digital Sky Survey has created the most detailed three-dimensional maps of the Universe ever made, with deep multi-color images of one third of the sky, and spectra for more than three million astronomical objects.

Designed to provide flexibility for teachers, with three distinct activity types including preflight, launch and expeditions to create a range of custom educational experiences.

MaNGA Data with Marvin
MaNGA is a wide-field IFU spectroscopic survey of 10,000 galaxies. Marvin is a tool suite designed for overcoming the challenge of searching, accessing, and visualizing the MaNGA data, via the Web or using Python tools.
MaNGA Stellar Library
MaNGA Stellar Library (MaStar) is a stellar spectral library with more than 9000 stars with a wide coverage of stellar parameters, high quality calibration, and identical observing setup as the MaNGA survey.
